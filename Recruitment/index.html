What We Look For

Prepared

Show up on time, know the fights, bring your raid kit, and keep your character gemmed, enchanted, and ready.

Communicative

Use Discord, answer raid signups, speak up when you need help, and keep officers posted about attendance changes.

Coachable

Take feedback well, review logs when needed, and keep improving without turning mistakes into drama.

Consistent

We value reliable players who can make raid nights regularly and give early notice when real life wins.

Team-minded

Respect loot council, help with mechanics, and care about clean kills more than personal meters.

Community fit

Bring a good attitude. Epic Chaos is social, Discord-active, and built to be welcoming to adults who want a steady guild home.
