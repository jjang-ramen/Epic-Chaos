What We Look For

Prepared

Show up on time, know the fights, bring your raid kit, and keep your character ready.

Communicative

Use Discord, answer signups, speak up when you need help, and keep officers posted.

Coachable

Take feedback well, review logs when needed, and keep improving without drama.

Consistent

Reliable raiders matter. Make raid nights regularly and give early notice when real life wins.

Team-minded

Respect loot council, help with mechanics, and care about clean kills more than personal meters.

Community fit

Bring a good attitude. Epic Chaos is social, Discord-active, and built as a steady guild home.

About Epic Chaos

Friends first, bosses second, still serious when the pull timer starts.

Epic Chaos welcomes new and veteran players alike. The guild runs normal, heroic, Mythic progression, Mythic+ keys, Glory runs after AOTC, mount and transmog runs, Timewalking raids when available, movie nights, other games, and random contests for store mounts, pets, gold, and prizes.

The guild is intentionally social: Discord matters, good attitudes matter, and the raid team wants people, not just bodies filling a slot. It is also called out as a safe place for women and LGBTQIA players.
